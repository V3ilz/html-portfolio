The Best Movies According to William

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Fight Club

This movie made me feel better about myself knowing that other people already
experienced psychosis as well; it was when I didn't really knew what it was all
about at the time.

Inception

I really liked the depth in this movie, a dream within a dream is an enjoyable
metaphor for life itself.

Good Will Hunting

From this point forward, I developed a special relationship with the matter of
psychology; through the represented relationship between Will and his therapist.
